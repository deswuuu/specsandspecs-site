About the Store

Fashion district roots.
Independent spirit.

Specs & Specs Optical has been part of Queen Street West since 1993. The store carries brand name and independent designer frames sourced from around the world — hand-selected by a buyer with a sharp eye for what actually works in Toronto's most fashion-forward neighbourhood.

01
A Curated SelectionThe team is intentional about what they stock. You'll find variety without the overwhelming volume of a chain — and staff who know every frame on the wall.
02
They'll Find What Works for Your FaceThe staff take time to understand what you actually want — working with you to find frames that suit you, not just what's popular.
03
Repairs WelcomeBring in your glasses and the team will handle adjustments, repairs, and lens replacements — quick and straightforward.
04
Multi-Lingual & Walk-In FriendlyNo appointment needed. Multi-lingual service available. Street and bike parking nearby.
